Shackled Shadow(Monogame C# Platformer Game)

You are a shadow who escaped the owner looking for freedom. Your former owner is chasing you down and you must avoid him. The game features two different worlds i.e., Shadow world and Real world. Travel between two worlds solving puzzles, fighting enemies with your powers. Explore the world and find your freedom

I am the sole developer and designer of this game made with monogame as part of the coursework. The game was built from scratch hence many game managers and systems needed to be coded they include

  • Start and End screen with recording high scores, level restart etc.
  • A innovative game mechanic where art switches(switching world) when a power up is used.
  • Level design extracted from txt files(You can modify the txt file and player, enemy or tile positions can be changed)
  • Power up drops with 2 variations.
